This app was inspired by a moments reminiscing about childhood memories. We had a moment to reflect on all the music, movies and moments that shaped our lives, and thought, "Why not make an app that gives people the same feeling?"

Better yet, why not engage people into building their own portfolio of memories, that includes things like the fashion, news (only the good kind of course), tv shows, and, our personal favorite, video games that were part of their childhood?
